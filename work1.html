About this Piece

The Ethereal Vase represents a harmonious blend of traditional porcelain craftsmanship and contemporary design sensibilities. Its graceful form emerges from a single piece of carefully thrown porcelain, featuring delicate transitions and subtle curves that catch and reflect light in mesmerizing ways.

The surface treatment combines smooth, polished areas with intentionally textured sections, creating an interplay of visual and tactile experiences. The translucent quality of the porcelain is enhanced by varying wall thickness throughout the piece.

Process

Each vase is individually thrown on the wheel and carefully refined over several days. The form undergoes multiple stages of refinement before the final firing at 1300°C (2372°F). The resulting piece exhibits the characteristic translucency and strength that makes porcelain such a remarkable material.
