About this Piece

The Zen Bowl embodies the principles of simplicity and mindfulness found in Japanese ceramics. Its generous form and subtle details create a sense of balance and tranquility, making it both a functional vessel and a contemplative object.

The custom glaze, developed through extensive experimentation, responds to the firing conditions to create unique patterns and textures that echo natural landscapes and weathered surfaces.

Process

This bowl is wheel-thrown using a specially prepared stoneware clay body. After initial forming, the piece is carefully trimmed to achieve its characteristic foot and profile. The custom glaze is applied in multiple layers and fired in a reduction atmosphere at 1260°C (2300°F), allowing the materials to interact and create depth and variation in the surface.
