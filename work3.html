About this Piece

Abstract Form explores the intersection of organic movement and geometric precision. This sculptural piece challenges traditional ceramic forms while maintaining a strong connection to the material's natural properties.

The dynamic composition creates different visual experiences from various angles, inviting viewers to move around the piece and discover new relationships between its forms and surfaces.

Process

This sculpture combines hand-building techniques with wheel-thrown elements. The complex form is assembled from multiple components, each carefully crafted and joined while maintaining the clay's optimal working properties. Multiple glazes are applied strategically to enhance the form's dynamic qualities and fired to 1280°C (2336°F) to achieve the desired surface effects.
